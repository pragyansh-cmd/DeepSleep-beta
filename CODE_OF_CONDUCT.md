# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want DeepSleep to be a welcoming project for builders, beginners, and contributors from different backgrounds.

Please:

- be respectful
- assume good intent
- give actionable feedback
- focus criticism on ideas and code, not people
- help keep discussions useful and kind

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- hate speech
- doxxing
- repeated bad-faith disruption
- sexualized language or unwanted advances

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that violate this policy.

For serious problems, contact the maintainer through GitHub.
